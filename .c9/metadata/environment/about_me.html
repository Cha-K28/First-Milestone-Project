Who am I?

\n\n

\n My name is Charlie King and I am from Ireland. I have had a very keen interest in cars and in particular the tecnology in cars since i was a teenager. I discovered ADAS about 5 years ago after driving a Volkswagen Golf equipped with Adaptive Cruise Control.\n I began to research more about ADAS and the different systems that are available.
