About Me

Who am I?

My name is Charlie King and I am from Ireland. I have had a very keen interest in cars and in particular the tecnology in cars since i was a teenager. I discovered ADAS about 5 years ago after driving a Volkswagen Golf equipped with Adaptive Cruise Control. I began to research more about ADAS and the different systems that are available. I even took a trip to the Geneva Car Show to discover the next generation on ADAS coming through.

As i began to learn more about ADAS, I realised that many people and car buyers didn't know anything about ADAS. ADAS can greatly improve the driving experience aswell as keep people safer on the road. I then decided to create this website to help to inform people about ADAS.
