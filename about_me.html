About Me

Who am I?

My name is Charlie King and i am from Ireland. I have had a very keen interest in cars and in particular the tecnology in cars since i was a teenager. I began to learn about ADAS about 5 years ago after driving a car equipped with Adaptive Cruise Control. I began to research more about ADAS and the different systems available today. I even took a trip to the Geneva Car Show to discover the next generation on ADAS coming through

As i began to learn more about ADAS i realised that most people and car buyers didn't know anything about ADAS and how it can benifit their driving experience aswell as keep them safer on the road. I then decided to create this website to help to inform people about ADAS.
