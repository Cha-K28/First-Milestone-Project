What is ADAS?

Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

ADAS ia an abbriviation for Advanced Driver Assistance Systems. ADAS is an evolution of older safety systems that are now standard features in most cars today such as ABS, Cruise Control, Parking Sensors and Automatic lights.
