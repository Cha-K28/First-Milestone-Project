Adaptive Cruise Control

Adaptive Cruise Contol is a more advanced version of regular cruise control you find in most cars today. Have you ever been driving along a motorway with your cruise control active when suddendy you realise you are very quickly getting to close to the vehicle travelling ahead of you? Well, Adaptive Cruise contro can help to prevent this! the Adaptive Cruise Control systems useally use a radar sensor at the front of the vehicle to monitor the traffic situation ahead.
