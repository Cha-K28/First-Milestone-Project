ADVANCED DRIVER ASSISTANCE SYSTEMS

ADAS

This site is about Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) as well as more of the latest technology found in todays cars. We will explain what ADAS is, the different ADAS available and how it can aid you driving every day. Hopefully it will show you the importance of having ADAS fitted to your next car. We also hope that this website will educate dealers and encourage vehicle manufacturers and dealers to upsell these ADAS features in cars and in turn make our roads safer.

Browse through the site to find out what ADAS is, where it began and what will come in the future!
