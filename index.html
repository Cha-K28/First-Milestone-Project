Welcome to this ADAS site!

This site will be based around Advance Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) as well as more of the latest technology found in todays cars. Over the next ten weeks we will explain what ADAS is, the different ADAS available and how it can aid you driving every day. Hopefully it will show you the importance of having ADAS fitted to your next car. We also hope that this series will educate dealers and encourage vehicle manufacturers and dealers to upsell these ADAS features in cars and in turn make our roads safer.

Browse through the site to find out what ADAS is, where it began and what will come in the future!
